Why Choose Us

Ben Bivins Tree Experts was established in 2012. We have become one of Ocean County’s top tree removal and tree care experts because of our professionalism, high level of skill, and customer care. But that’s not all! There are many reasons clients continue to choose us and recommend us to family and friends.

We Care About Trees

Trees provide many benefits to your home and yard. They can help to reduce energy bills by providing shade for your home in the summer and greatly improve & influence your curb appeal with the proper planting & care. In addition, they help clean the air and are important for our environment.  Our goal is to help every home in Ocean County care for their trees in the best way possible.  When your trees are healthy and strong, the benefits they provide are maximized.
