Pruning, Trimming & Crown Reduction

Trees and plants play a huge role in the curb appeal of your property. They also provide functional benefits such as offering shade on a hot summer day and play a vital role in creating oxygen through photosynthesis. For these reasons, it’s important to invest in your trees, plants and shrubs to ensure they remain healthy throughout the year.

Pruning and Trimming

The process of pruning involves assessing the health of a plant or tree, and performing selective removal of branches in the interest of the plant or tree’s health. Overgrown, damaged, or dead branches may be removed to allow a tree to promote proper growth.

Crown Reduction

Do you have a tree on your property that you love, but over time it has become overgrown and unsightly? Let our team of tree experts evaluate your trees and recommend a plan of action. Crown reduction, the process of reducing the tree’s canopy by removing specific branches, can make a world of difference! Let more sunlight into your yard, clean up low hanging branches and start enjoying your property again!
